Five accounts with both spend and SoI increasing: CenturyGames, Wedobest, Luckymoney, Aviagames, and 37GAMES.

Model & tooling improvements are real unlocks

  • IAA model upgrade (Wedobest): More IAA puzzle titles can now run and scale.
  • tKPI maturation: WoolCrush grew from test to ~7k DRR; Solitaire Clash UK moved from heavy under-spend to ~7–8k DRR.
  • Tool stack (Aviagames): CPI balancer + D1/D3 + traffic control allow image-heavy campaigns to win cheap inventory while staying within KPI.

Unlocking more supply via creative & geo changes

  • Aviagames — creative/inventory unlock: Previously, strict VT control + BI CPM<1 filter blocked high-value supply. GDS showed that on best publishers, images account for ~50%+ of spend/inventory. After removing filters, image supply visibility and ROI improved significantly.
  • CenturyGames — geo-driven supply unlock: Historically concentrated on T0/T1 markets. Now testing T2/T3 markets and RU variants to tap additional cheaper supply while keeping T0/T1 budgets running.
  • Wedobest — creative + geo broadening: Migrated from misleading to normal creatives. Geo ladder: KR/JP/TW → regional (EU/SEA) → worldwide, progressively opening more geos.

Key structural risk: concentration on a few hero titles

Per advertiser, 1–3 apps drive most DRR growth. If any hit a growth-factor, policy, or lifecycle issue, the entire account moves.

2. Cross-Account Prevention & Defence Factors

Fixing measurement and attribution bugs fast

  • CenturyGames: Fixed LAT revenue under-prediction, supply restrictions, attribution leakage, RMG mis-classification, and TCM/CPI oscillation. This protects large baseline DRR during T2/T3/RU and new title tests.

Framing lifecycle & channel roles clearly

  • Luckymoney / Winner: Position Moloco as strongest in launch and growth phases; Smartdex/ASA for mature budgets. This prevents a “Moloco underperforming” framing when share naturally shrinks post-Smartdex ramp.

Blueprints and playbooks for safe campaign structures

  • CenturyGames: Consolidation + overlap rules for P0 structure.
  • Aviagames / Solitaire Clash: Image-heavy tKPI + CPI balancer + D1/D3 + traffic control as the safe default config.
  • Wedobest: KR/JP/TW → regional → worldwide geo ladder as the default rollout.

Guardrails on creative & geo changes

  • Wedobest: Moved to normal creatives with before/after performance data. Enforcing the geo ladder prevents “tried once, failed” dismissals.

Policy and filter diagnostics before scale decisions

  • Aviagames: Top-publisher ROI + creative mix analysis proved VT + CPM<1 rules blocked high-value supply. Risk of reversion is lower post-monitoring.

Clear attribution of why metrics moved

  • Wedobest: Decomposed SoI gains to WaterSort/ScrewSort, and spend gains to Cell Survivor/WoolCrush + tKPI. Clean causal explanation versus black-box noise.

Codifying “what good looks like” early (reference curves)

  • Use early-phase DRR & KPI curves from Ten Luck, MU, Solitaire Clash UK, and WoolCrush (~7k DRR) as reference launch patterns for future titles.
