Premium Account SoI Report (2026-02-21 to 2026-02-27)

Summary and Messaging to GM leads:
DRR Trends: At the pod level, the average L7 DRR compared to the baseline (Dec 25 - Dec 31) shows strong upward momentum from Top Pod 2, Top Pod 3, and Growth Pod 2.

Overall, the average DRR is up by +0.52%. It is important to note that the baseline we use (the last week of December) was heavily inflated by an end-of-year uptick from Bitool, which accounted for \~31% of the total average DRR at that time. Bitool's share has since normalized to about 20% of our recent week's DRR.

If we remove the outsized impact of Bitool from the equation, we actually see a much stronger underlying growth rate of +16.1% across the remaining premium accounts compared to the baseline. Additionally, when zooming out to look at Bitool quarter-over-quarter, the account is still trending upward overall despite coming down from its late-December peak.

SoI Trends: From an SoI perspective, we are -0.87% under the baseline overall. Only Pod 3 saw an increase (+0.2%), driven by strong performance from Luckymoney, Lastwar, and Aviagames.

Conversely, some platforms (such as Century Games, TikTok, and Learnings) saw an increase in DRR alongside a decrease in SoI. This could indicate organic growth in the app's overall traffic or increased investment across other DSPs.

If anyone has intel directly from the advertisers regarding these shifts, we would love to hear it!
