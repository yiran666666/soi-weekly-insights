Weekly Update — March 30, 2026

Ads Spend

Key Milestones

  • Record Daily Revenue: Sunday hit $10.05M, marking the first-ever $10M+ day outside of a Super Bowl weekend. Friday ($9.99M) and Saturday ($9.79M) also saw near-record performance.
  • IAA Weekly Record: Weekly In-App Advertising (IAA) spend excluding Hungry Studio crossed $10M for the first time.

Non-IAA Performance (+9.0% W/W | +$4.2M)

Real Money Gaming (RMG) (+22.1% | +$2.4M)

  • Growth fueled by March Madness season.
  • Kalshi drove ~66% of this growth.
  • US accounts (Fanatics, DraftKings) contributed the remainder.
  • Polymarket reactivated at $70K/day after a 44-day pause.

Consumer (+12.4% | +$1.8M)

  • US drove 96% of the increase.
  • Pinterest contributed over half (+$964K).
  • Dentsu–Microsoft Copilot followed (+$344K).

Gaming

  • Spend remained flat week-over-week.

Connected TV (CTV) Performance (+34.1% W/W | +$241K)

  • Growth entirely driven by PMG–Fanatics capitalizing on March Madness.

IAA & Hybrid Performance (+9.4% W/W | +$1.3M)

  • Top Drivers: Bravestars led with 60% of the increase (+$770K), followed by Hungry Studio (+$150K).
  • App Ecosystem Growth:
    • Active IAA apps increased 9% to 707 total.
    • Apps spending >$1K/day increased 7% to 250 total.

SoI x Spend Update (W13 — as of Mar 26)

TL;DR: March (MTD) SoI is 7.49% (+0.12pp vs 7.37% baseline), the strongest month YTD. YTD average is 7.21% (−0.17pp), weighed down by the Feb dip. Gap to 9.37% target: +2.17pp (need +0.24pp/month over 9 remaining months).

Why the Gap

  • Moloco installs are −27% vs baseline (399K → 290K/day) while unattributed installs are +27% (19.0M → 24.1M/day).
  • Spend is healthy at $1,957K/day (+2.6% vs baseline). This is an install volume problem, not a spend problem.
